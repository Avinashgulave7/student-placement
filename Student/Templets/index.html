ABOUT US

Histroy of college .

  • Padmashri Vikhe Patil College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Pravaranagar, was established in June, 1971 under the aegis of Pravara Rural Education Society, Pravaranagar. The College is located in rural area in Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra and has been catering to the educational needs for the boys and girls of surrounding 44 villages. The college has around 52 Acres lush green and sprawling campus equipped with ultra-modern facilities for teaching, learning and research.
  • The college is permanently affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University,Pune and is approved u/s 2(f) and 12 (b) of the UGC act..
  • The college is ISO 9001-2008 certified. It is bestowed with Star College by DBT in 2010. The college was Accredited 'A+' grade and Re-Accredited 'A' grade with CGPA 3.61 by the NAAC in 2012 and has been recognized as the College of Excellence by UGC, New Delhi in 2014.
  • Along with conventional courses the college offers vocational courses like Seed Technology, Functional English & Computer Application.
  • The college has been supporting poor, intelligent and needy students through Earn and Learn Scheme since its inception.
  • Active placement and career guidance cell helps students in fetching on and off campus jobs. The extra-curricular activities like NSS and NCC are helpful in developing good citizens of India.
